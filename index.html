Graco Move With Me Soother Swing Stargazer

The Graco Move With Me Soother swings side to side, featuring 5-speed soothing motion and gentle vibrations to calm your little one in minutes. The multi-position recline seat creates the optimum environment for bottle feeding or nap time. This rocker has an audio system with 10 built-in melodies, 5 nature sounds. In addition, it supports phone or MP3 connection to play your baby's favourite songs. The infant insert with 3-point harness keeps your baby cosy and secure at all times.

The Graco Move comes with a canopy and integrated toy bar with two plush toys, which can be used as a carry handle. The compact size and battery operated option enable you to unplug the rocker's power cable to move the rocker around your house easily. A quick, two steps folding feature makes this rocker even more compact when it needs to be stored away.

Features:

  • Suitable from birth up to 9kg
  • Swaying motion gently floats from side to side, lulling your little one to sleep
  • Adjustable swaying speed - 5 different settings
  • Built-in vibration unit - 2 speed options
  • Audio system with 10 melodies and 5 nature sounds
  • MP3/Phone connectivity lets you to play baby's favourite songs
  • Integrated carry handle /toy bar with soft hanging toys for visual stimulation
  • The multi-position recline
  • Compact size is easy to carry and store away
  • Easy folding mechanism
  • Power (cable included) or battery operated

Dimensions: H68 x W51 x D72cm

Weight: 6.8kg
